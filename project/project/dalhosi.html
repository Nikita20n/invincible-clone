    Trek to Kalatop Basecamp

    Trek Time: 6-7 hours
    Altitude: 7,800 ft
    Trek Distance: 12 kms

    Kalatop is a well known hilltop village. More than 200 movies are shot here and the views from here are amazing. The height is approximately 8000 ft from the mean sea level.

    After breakfast, we will trek towards the North through dense forest. It is mainly made of Deodar and Pine trees. The area is rich with flora and fauna, there are many species of birds you can watch if you keep silent during the trek inside the Kalatop Forest. Our trek will often cross the main road and for almost 3 km before the Lakkad Mandi, we will be walking on roads only to enjoy the majestic views of Kalatop and the magical backdrop of the Himalayas.

    Lakkadmandi is a small village, where you can enjoy tea and biscuits, or a hot maggie. However, we will reach Kalatop Basecamp by lunch time.

    The best thing you could do at Kalatop is to experience the incredible sunset over the mountains. The colour changes from red to purple and slightly dips into dark blueâ€¦ it is kind of hypnotic.

    Enjoy hot dinner with your group members and then we will have a briefing session for the summit attempt. Sleep well for a better experience of the next day.
